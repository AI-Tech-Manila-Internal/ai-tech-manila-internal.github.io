About Us

AI & Tech Manila (AITM) is a community for people who want to understand AI trends, discuss new breakthroughs, study use cases, and meet other technology enthusiasts! We are software engineers, entrepreneurs, organizational leaders, AI enthusiasts, and students!

We recognize the monumental impact that AI will have on our society, and we bring together tech enthusiasts, futurists, and curious newcomers alike for thoughtful conversations on emerging AI capabilities and their impact on nearly every major aspect of our lives: from business, medicine, law, and engineering to how we interface with the internet/the cloud and with each other.

At the same time, we acknowledge the sobering consequences of AI misuse and rogue AI agents, as well as arguably some of the most challenging problems of our times: AI values alignment, causal reasoning, monitoring and regulation, and achieving AGI Emergence.

If we are to build a flourishing post-AI society, we must give these challenges the spotlight they deserve in public discourse. AITM hosts in-person presentations, discussions, and workshops designed to help people understand and adapt to fast-moving technological change. Our goal is simple: build a strong, open community where people can learn, share ideas, sharpen their thinking, and use technology in ways that are ethical, practical, and pro-human flourishing.

Our Team

We are a volunteer organization. AITM is built and run by people who care enough to give their time, energy, and ideas to grow a thriving tech community.

We hail from all over the globe: from New York to San Francisco, from Sydney to Manila. Our members come from just as many different backgrounds: engineering, startups, business, law, and finance. What unites us is a shared belief that the future of AI and technology will be transformational for our careers, the broader economy, and humanity’s future. Through our events, our goals are to:

  1. Instill a better understanding of AI news and trends
  2. Build skills to help our community thrive in the marketplace
  3. Learn to navigate technological trends and help shape positive change in an AI-dominant society
  4. Share & provide technology for the common good, in Service of Humanity
