About Us

AI & Tech Manila is a community for people who want to understand AI trends, discuss new breakthroughs, study use cases, and meet other technology enthusiasts — software engineers, entrepreneurs, leaders, and students.

We host in-person presentations, discussions, and workshops to help people understand and adapt to fast-moving technological change — ethically, practically, and pro-human.
