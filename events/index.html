Two Event Formats, One Community

We host monthly gatherings, alternating between two formats:

💬

Chill & Chat

A relaxed, small-group setting for 15–30 participants

Guided tech discussions on AI and technology trends

Open Q&A & member-driven topics

2–3 hours of meaningful conversation

Networking opportunities with like-minded peers

Perfect for: Deep discussions, building relationships, and exploring ideas in an intimate setting

🎤

Tech Spotlight

A speaker-driven event attracting 60–100 attendees

2–4 volunteer speakers per session

Topics spanning AI, software, hardware, and emerging tech

Engaging presentations and demonstrations

Networking sessions to spark collaboration

Perfect for: Learning from experts, discovering new technologies, and expanding your network
