About Tech Spotlight

A stage for the ideas shaping tech in Manila.

Got a story, an insight, or a build worth sharing? Tech Spotlight is our signature speaker series — highlighting the people and ideas pushing AI, startups, and emerging tech forward.

The Journey

From application to applause.

What happens after you hit submit — no mystery, no ghosting.

01

You apply

A quick form — title, abstract, who you are. Five minutes.

02

We review

Our team reads every submission and replies within a week — yes, no, or "let's refine."

03

We prep together

A short call to align on framing, slides if you want feedback, and run-of-show details.

04

You take the stage

15–30 minutes in front of an audience that actually wants to be there. We handle logistics.

05

The afterglow

Recording, social cuts, ongoing community access — the talk keeps working for you.
