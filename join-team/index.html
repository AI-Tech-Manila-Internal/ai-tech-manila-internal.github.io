Why join our team

A small team. A growing community. A real chance to shape it.

AI & Tech Manila started as a few friends and a WhatsApp group. Today, it's hundreds of people gathering every month — founders, engineers, students, and curious minds.

We're still tiny behind the scenes. If you're driven by community, collaboration, and the way technology brings people together, we'd love your help — and we'll make sure your work actually matters.

The Commitment

What we ask in return.

It's a volunteer role — so the bar is real ownership of a slice, not full-time hours.

Time commitment

~3 hrs / week

Plus the monthly event if you can make it. Mostly async — we don't pretend otherwise.

01

Own your slice

Run your area with judgment, not waiting for approval on every move.

02

Communicate honestly

If life gets busy, just say so. We'd rather you set a slower pace than burn out quietly.

03

Show up for the community

Help newcomers feel welcome. Curiosity over credentials, every time.

04

Bring your taste

Push back when something feels off. We hire volunteers for their judgment, not their politeness.
